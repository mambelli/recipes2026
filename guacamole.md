# Guacamole

## Ingredients

- avocado
- cilantro
- onions
- lime
- salt
- tomato

## Steps

1. First step is to cut the tomatos, cilantro, and onions into small bits
2. Next cut the avacado in half and remove the skin and seed from the fruit
3. Place the seedless and skinless avacado into a bowl and mash it
4. place salt, lime, onions, tomatos, and cilantro into the bowl and mix
5. Enjoy!
