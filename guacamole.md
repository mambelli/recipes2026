# Guacamole

## Ingredients

- avocado
- onions
- tomato
- garlic
- cilantro
- salt
- pepper
- lemon


## Instructions 
- Mash the avocado in a medium serving bowl.
- Stir in chopped onion, tomato, and minced garlic.
- Season with lime juice, salt, and pepper to taste.
- For a unique method, you can combine avocado flesh, garlic, lime juice, oil, salt, and pepper in a zip-lock bag, seal it, and mash to combine before adding tomatoes and onion
