## Rattatouille

Ratatouille is a French Provençal dish that showcases a variety of vegetables, including eggplant, zucchini, bell peppers, and tomatoes. While it might seem simple, the preparation requires careful slicing and layering to create a visually appealing dish. This recipe celebrates the freshness of seasonal vegetables and can be served as a main or side dish. The complexity comes from the different flavors and textures that develop as the vegetables cook down.

### Ingredients:
- Eggplant
- Zucchini
- Bell Peppers
- Tomatoes
- other Assorted of Food
- flavors and textures
